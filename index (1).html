🏆 Know Your Competition

Before you post a single thing, understand exactly where you stand against your local competitors — their SEO, their content gaps, their ad strategy, and what it means for yours.

💡 Why This Comes First

Most business owners guess at what to post. The ones who win search for exactly what their competitors are ranking for — and then do it better. This analysis gives you a complete picture of your local competitive landscape across SEO, AEO, GEO, and SGE so every piece of content you create is strategically targeted.

Scanning your local competitive landscape...
📊 Your Competitive Position
🔎 SEO — Search Engine Optimization
What Google finds when someone searches for your type of business in your city. This tells you which keywords your competitors are winning and which ones you can take from them.
🤖 AEO — Answer Engine Optimization
AI assistants like Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant, and ChatGPT pull from specific sources to answer questions like "what is the best coffee shop near me." This shows whether AI engines know your business exists — and what they are saying about your competitors.
📍 GEO — Generative Engine Optimization
When someone asks an AI to recommend local businesses, the AI generates its answer from training data and live search. GEO is about making sure AI systems recommend YOU — not your competitors. This shows where you stand and what to do about it.
✨ SGE — Search Generative Experience
Google now shows an AI-generated summary at the top of search results before any website links. Your competitors whose content Google chooses for these summaries get free visibility without users clicking. This shows who is winning those spots and how you can too.

What your top competitors are posting on social media, which content is performing best, and exactly what gaps they are leaving open for you to fill.
